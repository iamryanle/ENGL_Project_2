CONCLUSION



Authors of electronic literature must and do take into account the different forms of user interaction in electronic literature. Electronic literature immerses the reader, activating the reader’s senses and initiating their own involvement and decision making in the narrative. This makes the reader vital to the way a narrative plays out, creating a unique meaning and experience for the individual. Thus, indubitably, interacting with a piece of electronic literature is key for the reader’s experience with any piece of electronic literature. Interacting with different pieces creates a vivid and engaging experience that cannot be achieved with traditional literature. The users can only interact by reading, limiting the meaning and experience for the reader.
